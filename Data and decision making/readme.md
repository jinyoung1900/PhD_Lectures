This is a folder that hosts all my works in data and decision making. 
link to the course description: https://opas.peppi.oulu.fi/en/course/721022S/16824?period=2024-2025 

Quote: 
The course gives an overview of data-based decision making in an organizational and network-driven context, addressing both key organizational functions, stakeholders, resources and transactions. External market-driven needs regarding business strategy, business model and earning logic are included, as well as how customer, supplier and competitor data can be utilized. An overview of the key business processes and support systems is given, from the viewpoint of business planning, execution and improvement. The needs of small and medium-size companies, public service organizations and community-based business settings are also brought up.
